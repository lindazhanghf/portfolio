Haunted

Haunted

Final Project of Game Design

Huanted is the game I made for the final project for game design class. It features a first person controller in a 3D environment.

In this game, the player plays a ghost that is haunting this house in the middle of the woods. Using ghost power to interact with objects in the house, the player tries to scare the man reading by his bookshelf. The goal is to scare the man out of his house.

  • Created in Unity3D
  • Multiple Interaction with Environment
  • AI Character Design
  • Sound Effects

Game AI Design

State Machine

Using state machine to controll the movement of NPC over time. The red bar on left side of the screen indicates the current "scared level" of the NPC, corresponding to the state of NPC. There are total of 5 states for the NPC:

  • Idle: calmly reading books at first
  • Curious: inspecting objects
  • Scared: scared away from the floating objects
  • Running: running around the house
  • Exiting: exiting out of the house
Idle State:
NPC standing next to the bookshelf
Curious State:
NPC running towards the floating object
Scared State:
NPC running away to avoid the floating object
Running State:
NPC running and screaming all over the house like crazy
Exiting State:
NPC screaming and rushing out of the house (player won)
